    Tools: Mathematica, Magma, LMFDB

  • MATH 7390: Representation Theory and Automorphic Forms

    Explored deep connections between the representation theory of SL(2,R) and automorphic forms on the upper half-plane. Investigated the structure of Lie groups via Casimir operators and bi-invariant differential operators and discussed the Iwasawa and Bruhat decompositions. Here is the project.
  • MATH 4901: Directed Reading Program on L-Functions

  • ORIE 4999: Polytopes
    Fall 2023 | Cornell University
    Conducted literature review on Polynomial Hirsch Conjecture and Barnette’s Conjecture. Analyzed the combinatorial and geometric properties of polytopes and convex hulls, with attention to counterexamples and their implications in linear programming.

    Outcome: Assesses progress toward resolving two major open problems in discrete geometry and optimization theory.

  • NSF-REU: Numerical Methods for Optical Design
    Summer 2023 | University of Rochester
    Modeled freeform optical surfaces using orthogonal polynomial interpolation (Legendre and Chebyshev bases). Investigated convergence behavior and surface-fitting precision for lens geometries. Presented findings to faculty and peers.

    Tools: MATLAB
    Impact: Supports design of high-precision optical systems in imaging and photonics.

  • ORIE 4999: Optimizing University Course Scheduling
    Spring 2023 | Cornell University
    Led a team to develop a large-scale linear program for optimizing university-wide course scheduling. Integrated course, instructor, room, and other constraints using real administrative datasets.

    Tools: Python, Excel
    Impact: Solution is currently used by the Registrar for scheduling; findings presented at the Cornell Undergraduate Research Symposium.
